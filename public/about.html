Who We Are

Wizard Make Potion is a ticketing platform created by Blake and his friends to sell tickets for DJ events that they put on. We're a group of music lovers and event organizers passionate about creating unforgettable experiences on the dance floor.

What started as a way to manage our own events has grown into a full-featured ticketing system that handles everything from payments to door check-ins.

What We Do

We organize and host DJ events featuring local and touring artists. Our platform makes it easy to:

  • Browse upcoming DJ events and performances
  • Purchase tickets securely with instant confirmation
  • Receive QR code tickets via email
  • Quick check-in at the door with mobile tickets
